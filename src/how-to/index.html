Training Hub

Quick tips to help you film better polling-location explainers and earn points the right way.

1) The 30–60 Second Formula

  1. Say where you are (name + address).
  2. Say what the video is for: “This is where you vote in the primary.”
  3. Explain early voting: Feb 17 – Mar 3.
  4. Explain primary vs. nonpartisan (simple and clear).
  5. Close with: “Vote for Chris Jones for Congress.”

2) Camera Basics (Fast)

  • Film vertical (9:16).
  • Stand in good light and keep your face visible.
  • Keep background noise low if possible.
  • Speak slowly and clearly.

3) Submission Checklist

  • Post publicly.
  • Include required hashtags.
  • Tag the campaign.
  • Submit the link(s) through your Team Portal.
  • Claim only what you included.
